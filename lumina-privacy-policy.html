Lumina: Inspiration & Quotes - Mobile Application Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Lumina: Inspiration & Quotes ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Lumina does not collect any personal information and does not share any information with third parties.

2. Information We Collect

Lumina: Inspiration & Quotes does not collect any personal information. The app is designed to work completely offline and does not require an account, registration, or any personal details to function.

The app may store your favorite quotes and preferences locally on your device for your convenience. This information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any external servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to use, process, or share. The app functions entirely on your device using locally stored content.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data associated with Lumina: Inspiration & Quotes is stored locally on your device only. This includes any favorite quotes or preferences you may save. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any external services. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information.

5. Third-Party Services

Lumina: Inspiration & Quotes does not share any information with third parties. We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or any other third-party services that would collect or receive your data.

The app is distributed through app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play Store) which have their own privacy policies. However, we do not provide any user data to these platforms beyond what is required for app distribution.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to access, correct, delete, or port. All data stored by the app (favorite quotes, preferences) is stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Lumina: Inspiration & Quotes is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is completely safe for children to use. There is no risk of personal information being collected from children or anyone else, as the app does not have the capability to collect such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
